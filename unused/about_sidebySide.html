Morgan Escalera

born and raised in Boston, Massachusetts, is a single, caring father to his only daughter Lily. Morgan is forty-seven years old and his daughter Lily is six. Lilly’s mother, Elizabeth, recently passed away during a car crash on her way to work as a nurse in Boston’s Children’s Hospital. His wife Elizabeth saw his passion for politics and pushed him to be the best. After her death, he decided to finally take her advice and run for the presidency.

Morgan graduated from East Boston High School in 1985. After which he attended California Institute of Technology majoring in law. Then after his graduation in 1982, he served in the peace corps. After his service in the peace corps, he served two full terms as a democratic senate minority leader and is currently halfway through his third.

Steven Doolan

born and raised in Hyde Park, Ohio. At thirty-five years of age he has made quite a name for himself. He is single, but he says that he is married to his job. Steven attended St. Xavier High School and then attended Northwestern University, majoring in software engineering. In his free time, Steven loves to spend time outdoors fishing and hiking.

After he graduated from Northwestern University he was recruited by homeland security and worked there for six years. During this time he focused on cyber terrorism and saw improvements that he would have liked to have made to the program, however, he lacked the power to do so. Because of this, Steven developed a dream of being able to make a difference for the United States. He saw that his best chance to make a difference was in politics. Since he aspired to be involved in politics, he served one full term in the house majority and is currently halfway through his second.
