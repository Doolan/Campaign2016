Exclusive Interview

With William Foster

"As many of you know, Presidential Hopeful Senator Escalera has dropped out of the presidential race. He choose Senator Emily Bochnowski as his replacement. I decided to sit down with Senator Bochnowski (And, later on, Senator Doolan) and find out a bit more on her political beliefs..."

"Bochnowski stated that see agrees with the Roe v. Wade and Planned Parenthood v. Casey decisions, in that, abortions fall under a woman’s right to privacy. She continues with saying that no one can say “We know what you’re going through” unless they've gone through it themselves..."

"No matter what degree a person may have, Bochnowski wants them to be working, not unemployed..."

"Overall, it seems that Senator Bochnowski will have a very easy time with this campaign. The Bochnowski/Doolan campaign, unsurprisingly, seems very middle of the road, which appeals to me, personally. I think they'll be able to bridge the gap between Republicans and Democrats, and, hopefully, get both sides working for a better tomorrow."


-Willam Foster (Moderate) Read More
